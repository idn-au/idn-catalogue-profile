IDN Catalogue Profile

This is the definitional document of a Profile of cataloguing, provenance and vocabulary information standards designed for the representation of indigenous datasets and their mode of governance.

The formal metadata for this Profile is given below, including links to the machine-readable version of this document.

Metadata Requirements:
If you want to know what the requirements for information are to be conformant to this profile, see the Specification Document resource.

The Indigenous Data Network (IDN) is within the University of Melbourne. It was established in 2018 to support and coordinate the governance of Indigenous data for Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples and empower Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander communities to decide their own local data priorities.
